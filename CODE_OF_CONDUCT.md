# Code of Conduct

Hey there!

We are **Final Year IT students** from **Government Engineering College, Gandhinagar**. We built **Rollout.io** as our academic project to learn about distributed systems, microservices, and software engineering.

Since we are beginners exploring complex architectures, we expect anyone interacting with this project to keep the environment friendly, supportive, and constructive!

## The Golden Rules

1. **Be Nice & Respectful**: We are all here to learn. If you find a bug, a poorly optimized query, or a silly mistake in our code, feel free to point it out or fix it—but please be polite!
2. **Help Us Learn**: If you are a senior developer or a fellow student looking at our code, constructive feedback and architectural advice are highly appreciated.
3. **No Trolling**: We won't tolerate any abusive language, personal attacks, or harassment in the Issues section, Pull Requests, or Discussions. 
4. **Collaboration over Competition**: If you want to use our code for your own learning or extend it, you are more than welcome. Just give us a shoutout!

## Enforcement

Since this is a student-led project, the core team (Parthsinh, Dharmik, and Meet) will directly manage the repository. If someone violates these simple rules, we reserve the right to close their issues, block their pull requests, or report them to GitHub.

Let's code, learn, and grow together!
