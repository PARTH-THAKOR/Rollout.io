# Support and Help

Need assistance with Rollout.io? 

Since this is a final-year IT capstone project built by students at Government Engineering College, Gandhinagar, we want to ensure you have a smooth experience exploring our platform.

---

## Support Channels

### 1. GitHub Issues
For bugs, error traces, or technical platform issues:
- Please check existing issues first.
- If you cannot find a solution, open a new issue detailing the error and providing logs.

### 2. Email Support
For direct inquiries, architectural questions, or integration guidance:
- Contact us at: rollout@paraglide.in
- We aim to respond within 24 to 48 hours, depending on our coursework and examination schedules.
